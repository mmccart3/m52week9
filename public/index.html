My Camino de Santiago Journey

Hello there! I'm Mark, and I have a deep love for the Camino de Santiago. This ancient pilgrimage has woven its way into the fabric of my life, and I want to share my journey with you.

The Camino de Santiago, also known as the Way of St. James, is not just a physical journey; it's a spiritual and cultural adventure. The miles I've walked, the people I've met, and the breathtaking landscapes I've encountered have left an indelible mark on my soul.

Whether it's the rustic charm of the albergues, the camaraderie of fellow pilgrims, or the quiet moments of reflection in ancient churches, each step on the Camino tells a unique story.

Join me as I reminisce about the ups and downs, the blisters and laughter, and the profound moments of self-discovery along the Camino de Santiago.
